# The Omen (1976)

The Omen combines religious horror and suspense to create a chilling cinematic experience. Its atmosphere and score make it an enduring classic.

**Key Takeaways**

* Iconic horror with a compelling story of a child Antichrist.
* Gregory Peck delivers a strong, tense performance as the father.
* Jerry Goldsmith’s score remains one of the most memorable in horror history.
* Combines suspense, religious horror, and classic ’70s cinematic tension.

> “Some children are born to evil.”
